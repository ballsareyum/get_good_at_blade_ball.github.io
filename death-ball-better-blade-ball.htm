Is Death Ball Better Than Blade Ball Roblox?

So, you are a fan of Blade Ball? Well, that might not be the case for long—Death Ball is here!
By Tristan Wilkins | 
First Published November 5, 2023, 11:06

The fan-favorite Blade Ball might have a new challenger—Death Ball! Speaking of which, some Roblox players say that Death Ball is better than Blade Ball. They are both fantastic and fun games in their own right.

But there can only be one that stands above all. So, is Death Ball better than Blade Ball? Well, that's why we are here. Join us as we break it down for you! We will do our best to make a valid case for both games.

Editor's Note: Looking for Death Ball codes? We've got you covered! Check out our dedicated codes page for even more Roblox redeem codes!

Is Death Ball Better Than Blade Ball Roblox?

The simple answer is yes. Death Ball is better than Blade Ball! There are many reasons for this, though, which we will jump into right now. For starters, although Blade Ball is a great game, it can become boring pretty fast. Why? Because you do the same thing every game!

So, how is Death Ball different from Blade Ball? Well, I'm glad you asked! The first difference is that not only can you deflect the ball normally, but off of walls, too! That's right, you can deflect the ball off multiple surfaces to make awesome plays and catch your opponents off guard.

You get to use extra abilities and get special swords to help with deflecting at greater speeds and with different buffs. The stakes are also higher and more intense, with bigger arenas to play in and many possibilities for deflecting the ball. Death Ball is just better!

Also, there are active leaderboards, so you can track your progress on the way to the top! Death Ball is more intense and more fun than Blade Ball across the board.

If you like intense games that reward skillful plays and quick thinking, then Death Ball is the game for you! So get some friends together and duke it out in the highly exciting Death Ball!
